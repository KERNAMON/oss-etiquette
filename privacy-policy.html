Privacy Policy for OSS Etiquette v1.0 Adopters

Effective Date: December 12, 2024

Privacy Policy outlines how we handle personal data when contributors submit pull requests to have their projects or organizations listed on the OSS Etiquette v1.0 Adopters page, hosted on Cloudflare Pages.

1. Information We Collect

By submitting a pull request to our GitHub repository, you are voluntarily providing your GitHub Username, Project or Organization Name, Repository or Website Link, also your Contribution Message or Description.

2. How We Use This Information

Information submitted will be used to display your project or organization on the OSS Etiquette v1.0 Adopters webpage, provide attribution and maintain transparency.

3. Public Display of Information

All information submitted via pull requests is publicly visible on GitHub and the OSS Etiquette v1.0 Adopters page. By submitting a pull request, you consent to this public display.

4. Your Control Over Your Data

You retain full control over the data that you have voluntarily submited and you can request modifications or its removal at any time, by contacting us.

5. Data Retention

Your contribution will remain publicly displayed until you request its removal OR your data is no longer acurate, up-to-date and reliable. According to GDPR, the processing of personal data must be adequate, relevant and limited to what is necessary in relation to the specific purposes of the processing.

Conclusion

By following these guidelines, you can contribute positively to OSS Communities, build relationships and increase the chances of your contributions being well-received by others.
